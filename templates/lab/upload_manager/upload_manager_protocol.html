• Upload Manager useage guide:

    • Previewing Deletions

      1. Select "Preview the upload to be deleted" as well as the appropiate UploadBatch
      2. Enter the correct lab key (contact slin63@illinois.edu if you're not sure where to find it.)
      3. Hit submit and you should begin downloading a .csv containing all the objects in your selected UploadBatch

    • Deleting Batches

      1. Select "Delete the selected upload" as well as the appropiate UploadBatch
      2. Enter the correct lab key
      3. Give a short explanation for why you're deleting these items
      4. Check the checkbox and hit submit
